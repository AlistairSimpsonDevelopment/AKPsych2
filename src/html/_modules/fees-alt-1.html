Fees & Terms

Psychological Counselling Sessions are 50 minutes (a ‘therapeutic hour’) in length. Double sessions are sometimes available on request. Sessions are held at a regular time slot on a weekly basis which is agreed at the first appointment.

The fee for Individual Psychological Counselling is £100 per therapeutic hour. This is payable by cash or credit/debit card at the appointment. Payment is also available via insurance (Vitality Health & Aviva).

An initial 15 minute telephone consultation is offered free of charge to provide the opportunity for you to ask any questions you may have, and establish whether you would like to book an appointment. .
